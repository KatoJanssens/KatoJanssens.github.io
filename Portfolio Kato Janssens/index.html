Hi, my name is Kato Janssens and I’m living life as a graphic designer. Not just as my job, but actually living it.
I walk to the store worrying about the kerning on signage, I seem only to choose food with nice packaging. I feel bad for people who have unattractive flyers for their company – thinking about how I could redesign it. I look at a logo and think about contacting them about it. Should I...?

I know, now you're hesitating and thinking "Is she talking about our flyers?" "Do we have a bad logo?". I don't know but maybe we should meet. ;-)
